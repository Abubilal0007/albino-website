Raising Awareness for Albino Animals

Help us spread the word about the unique challenges albino animals face in the wild and captivity.

Visual Vulnerabilities

Albino animals often suffer from poor eyesight or complete blindness, making survival in the wild extremely challenging.

Sensitivity to Sunlight

Without protective pigments, albino animals are extremely sensitive to sunlight, making them prone to skin diseases and sunburns.
